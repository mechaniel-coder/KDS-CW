About Kidz Dat Shr3d

KDS is a family-run 501(c)(3) nonprofit built around one idea: every kid deserves a safe place to ride, land, and belong.

Kidz Dat Shr3d was founded in Pahrump, NV by Gary and Liza Kalawaia, who set out to give local kids somewhere to skate, bike, and scoot that felt like home. What began as informal backyard sessions has grown into an organized nonprofit serving young riders across the community.

Their son Kingsten mentors many of the younger riders who come through the program — teaching fundamentals, spotting tricks, and showing newcomers that progression takes patience as much as guts.

Each year, KDS hosts Shred Fest, a community fundraiser that brings riders, families, and local businesses together for a day of competition and camaraderie. Proceeds from Shred Fest — along with shop sales and individual donations — go directly toward gear, coaching, and the long-term goal of a dedicated, weather-proof action-sports facility for Pahrump youth.
